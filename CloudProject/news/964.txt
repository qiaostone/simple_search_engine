2 dead, 12 injured in slide accident in Chengdu
 (CRI Online)    13:12, May 02, 2019
	Two people are dead and 12 others are injured after falling off a giant slide in Chengdu, Sichuan Province on Wednesday, said local authorities early on Thursday.
	The accident happened at around 5 p.m. at an outdoor park leaving 14 people injured, including 8 adults and 6 minors.
	Two adults died after being taken to hospital. The other injured people have been hospitalized and are receiving medical treatment.
	The park has been ordered to halt operation. Further investigation is underway.
