Chinese Language Day event held at UN Office at Vienna, Austria
 (Xinhua)    16:38, May 07, 2019

	Acrobats from central China's Henan Province perform during the Chinese Language Day event at the United Nations Office at Vienna, Austria, on May 6, 2019. The event showed a variety of traditional Chinese painting and calligraphy, exhibits, and performance of opera and acrobatics. (Xinhua/Guo Chen)
