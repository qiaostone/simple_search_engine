Big-data based monitoring system helps guarantee food safety for students in Tongren
 (Xinhua)    08:31, May 22, 2019

	Aerial photo shows a staff member picking vegetables in Tongren, southwest China's Guizhou Province, May 21, 2019. Tongren City has established a monitoring system applying new technologies of big data to trace food from farming to dining table, an effort to guarantee food safety for students. (Xinhua/Ou Dongqu)
