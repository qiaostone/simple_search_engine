Farmers work on day of traditional Chinese solar term Xiaoman
 (Xinhua)    07:53, May 22, 2019

	A farmer checks the growth of wheat in a field in Wenxian County, central China's Henan Province, May 21, 2019. Traditional Chinese solar term Xiaoman (grain buds) falls on May 21 this year, which means seeds from grain are becoming full. (Xinhua/Xu Hongxing)
