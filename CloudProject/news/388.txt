Chinese marines conduct vessel inspection drill
 (CRI Online)    15:03, May 06, 2019

	Two yachts with marines of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) Navy meet on the sea during a vessel inspection drill on May 2, 2019 as part of the Sino-Russia Joint Sea 2019 naval exercise. China and Russia held the joint naval exercise from April 29 to May 4. [Photo: China Plus/Zhang Jin]
