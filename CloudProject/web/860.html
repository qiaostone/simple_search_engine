In pics: various plants at the Botanic Garden for Beijing International Horticultural Exhibition 2019

By Xian Jiangnan, Kou Jie, Morag Hobbs (People's Daily Online)    16:11, April 30, 2019

Photo taken on April 29, 2019 shows rare plants at the Botanic Garden for Beijing International Horticultural Exhibition 2019. (People's Daily Online/Xian Jiangnan)

Covering an area of about 10,000 square meters, the Botanic Garden for Beijing International Horticultural Exhibition 2019 houses over 1,000 plants. From mangrove forests to insectivorous plants, from poison arrow trees to miracle berries, the Garden shows an interesting and incredible plant kingdom.

The Botanic Garden also presents an interactive digital exhibition on its first floor, featuring a life-sized mangrove forest, microorganisms and an underwater world. The mind-blowing exhibition presents to the tourists a beautiful world with eye-catching ecological wonders.
