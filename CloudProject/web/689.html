"Dine Around Downtown" event held in New York, U.S.

(Xinhua)    10:26, May 22, 2019

People attend the "Dine Around Downtown" event in New York, the United States, on May 21, 2019. Dine Around Downtown has brought the neighborhood's restaurateurs together for the annual lunchtime festival since 1995. This year's event brought together nearly 40 restaurants in Lower Manhattan.
