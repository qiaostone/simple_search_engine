Activities held in kindergarten to greet upcoming Int'l Children's Day in China's Shanxi

(Xinhua)    09:32, May 23, 2019

Children and parents make handicrafts at the Liuyi Kindergarten in Wanrong County of Yuncheng, north China's Shanxi Province, May 22, 2019. Activities were held on Wednesday in Liuyi Kindergarten to greet the upcoming International Children's Day. (Xinhua/Cao Yang)
