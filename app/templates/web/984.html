The media center of the Conference on Dialogue of Asian Civilizations (CDAC) started trial operation at the China National Convention Center in Beijing on May 12, 2019.

China will hold the CDAC on May 15. Covering an area of 11, 000 square meters, the media center will become a platform for high-tech products.
