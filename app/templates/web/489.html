China-Russia joint naval exercise concludes in Qingdao

(Xinhua)    08:28, May 06, 2019

Photo taken on May 4, 2019 shows the naval forces paticipating the "Joint Sea-2019" exercise. A six-day joint naval exercise held by Chinese and Russian navies concluded Saturday in Qingdao, east China's Shandong Province. Two submarines, 13 surface ships, as well as fixed-wing airplanes, helicopters and marines participated in the exercise. (Xinhua/Li Ziheng)
