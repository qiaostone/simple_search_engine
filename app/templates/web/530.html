Afghan army soldiers take part in graduation ceremony in Kandahar

(Xinhua)    11:19, April 16, 2019

Afghan army soldiers take part in their graduation ceremony in Kandahar province, southern Afghanistan, April 14, 2019. A total of 993 army officers and soldiers graduated from an Afghan military training center and were commissioned to the army on Sunday, according to a statement. (Xinhua/Sanaullah Seiam)
