People visit European institutions' buildings in Brussels during Open Day

(Xinhua)    08:35, May 05, 2019

Girls visit the Berlaymont building, the European Commission headquarters, during the Open Day of the European institutions in Brussels, Belgium, May 4, 2019. The Open Day is a unique opportunity for the public to discover how the European institutions operate. Visitors have free access to the institutions' buildings and some special introductory activities. (Xinhua/Zheng Huansong)
