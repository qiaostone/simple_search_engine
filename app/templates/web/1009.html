Song and dance ensemble of Lhasa introduces culture and art to countryside

(Xinhua)    15:46, May 10, 2019

Actors and staff members of the song and dance ensemble of Lhasa perform at Jiaru Village in Lhasa, capital of southwest China's Tibet Autonomous Region, May 9, 2019. The ensemble gave performance to introduce the culture and art to countryside. More than 40 performers participated in 32 shows which attracted over 50,000 audiences from March 28 to May 9. (Xinhua/Li Xin)
