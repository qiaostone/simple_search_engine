Children make handicrafts at kindergarten in China's Zhejiang

(Xinhua)    09:49, May 23, 2019

Children cut patterns to make handicrafts at a kindergarten of Lijiaxiang Township, Changxing County, east China's Zhejiang Province, May 22, 2019. The kindergarten prepared different materials and designs for children to make handicrafts themselves. (Xinhua/Xu Yu)
